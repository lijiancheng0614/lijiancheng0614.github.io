Hindsight bias 后视偏差

HindSight bias, or the "I knew-it-all-along effect," is the tendency to exaggerate, after learning an outcome, one's ability to have foreseen the outcome.

后视偏差，也叫“我早就知道嘛”效应，指人们的认识有这样一种趋势，在知道了事情的结果后，事情理解起来似乎更容易了。

自我理解，社会关系，群体间关系，群体表现，决策制定，生活满足感，以及社会生活的其他很多方面。

Lecture 1.4: The Psychological Construction of Reality (12:39)

Change blindness 变化盲视

Change blindness is a surprising perceptual phenomenon that occurs when a change in a visual stimulus is introduced and the observer does not notice it.

变化盲视是指当观察者关注某个视觉刺激时，其它地方发生变化却不会注意到。

Motion aftereffect 运动后效

The motion aftereffect (MAE) is a visual illusion experienced after viewing a moving visual stimulus for a time (tens of milliseconds to minutes) with stationary eyes, and then fixating a stationary stimulus.

运动后效是指注视一个移动的视觉刺激并保持眼睛不动（几十毫秒到几分钟不等），一段时间后将眼睛注视到静止的物体上， 在大脑内产生的一种视觉错觉现象。

Lecture 1.5: Confirmation Bias (8:48)

Peter Wason's Four-Card Problem

Each card has a letter on one side and a number on the other.

If a card has a vowel on one side, then it has an even number on the other side.

每张卡片有一面是字母，有一面是数字。

如果一张卡片的一面是元音字母，那么它的另外一面就是偶数。

给定4张卡片：A，D，4，7

那么需要翻开A，7才能验证该命题是否为假。

因为原命题与逆否命题是等价的，因此需要同时验证A，7才能保证原命题为真。（若A的反面不是偶数，或7的反面是元音字母，则说明原命题为假。）

Confirmation bias 证实性偏见

A tendency to search for and weigh information that confirms one's preconceptions more strongly than information that challenges them.

人们普遍偏好能够验证假设的信息，而不是那些否定假设的信息。

Lecture 1.6: Self-Fulfilling Prophecies (11:51)

Self-fulfilling prophecy 自我实现预言

A belief that leads to its own fulfillment.

自我实现预言，又称自证预言，指人们先入为主的判断，无论其正确与否，都将或多或少的影响到人们的行为，以至于这个判断最后真的实现。

Pygmalion effect 皮革马利翁效应

The Pygmalion effect, or Rosenthal effect, is the phenomenon whereby higher expectations lead to an increase in performance.

皮格马利翁效应，指人在被付予更高期望以后，他们会表现的更好的一种现象。

Security dilemma 安全困境

The security dilemma, also referred to as the spiral model, is a term used in international relations and refers to a situation in which actions by a state intended to heighten its security, such as increasing its military strength or making alliances, can lead other states to respond with similar measures, producing increased tensions that create conflict, even when no side really desires it.

安全困境，也称为螺旋模型，是在国际关系中的术语，指在提高安全措施的行动时，如增加军事力量或结盟等，会导致其他国家应对类似措施，产生急剧冲突，然而任何一方可能都不想导致紧张局势加剧。

Behavioral Confirmation 行为确证

A type of self-fulfilling prophecy in which people's social

expectations lead them to behave in ways that cause others to confirm their expectations.

行为确证 是 自我实现预言 的一种，是人们的社会期望导致他们表现出特定的行为，而这种行为又会令他人表现得符合他们的期望。

Thin slice 薄片撷取

Thin-slicing is a term used in psychology and philosophy to describe the ability to find patterns in events based only on "thin slices," or narrow windows, of experience.

A meta-analysis is a statistical technique that combines and analyzes the results from different studies (literally, an analysis of analyses).

元分析是一种统计学技术，综合和分析不同研究的结果，顾名思义，元分析是对分析的分析。

Some Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Social judgments can take place very rapidly, sometimes with surprising accuracy.

社会判断的产生非常迅速，有时比我们预想得要准确

  • At the same time, they're prone to certain biases and distortions, and once our judgments are formed, we tend to lock into them.

同时，它们也容易受特定的偏见和歪曲影响，并且判断一旦形成，无论对错，我们倾向于锁定它。

并寻找支持性证据，而不会去怀疑已经形成的判断。
